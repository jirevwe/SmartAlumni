Frequently Asked Questions

What is Smart Alumni App?

Smart Alumni is a mobile App purposefully developed for school’s alumni to stay connected and give back to their schools.


What does Smart Alumni App do?

Smart Alumni brings together ex class and school mates onto one platform, enabling them administer alumni activities and fund projects for the benefit of their schools.


Why do I need the Smart Alumni App?

  • Old friendships from schools are re-kindled, new bonds are formed and the next generation is empowered.

  • Smart Alumni has a secure e-payment feature that assures donations are received directly by schools and/or their projects.


How do I download the Smart Alumni App?

The Smart Alumni can be downloaded directly from the Google Play Store. Alternatively, you can follow the link on our homepage.


What devices can I use the app on?

Smart Alumni is currently available on devices that run the Android operating system.


Can I connect my Smart Alumni account to my other social networks?

No. Your Smart Alumni account does not connect to other social media feed.


How do I update my contact information?

You can update your contact information on your profile page. Just click on edit, make your changes and don’t forget to save.


How do I retrieve my password if I forget it?

Use the “forgot password” link. We’ll ask for your email address to verify your identity and send you a link to reset your password via email.


How do I join a set?

Look up your school under the school tab, select the year in which you graduated and click join.


Is there a limit to the number of projects I can join?

No! If you wish to you can be involved and contribute to more than one project you can. Just ensure that you have signed in your school and/or joined a set.


Why am I unable to view a project?

You may not have joined the project you wish to see, or you may not be a member of the set endowing said project. Please ensure that the project is endowed by your set before trying to join.


What is the poll feature?

The poll feature is a tool useful for voting on issues members of any group and/or set find relevant.

How do I turn on and off Notifications?

  • Click on the Menu button

  • Select the Settings button and Click on Notification button to turn it on or off


How do I change my Password?

  • Click on Menu button

  • Select Settings button

  • Click on change password

  • Input Old Password, New Password and Confirm password

  • Click on Save Changes


How do I Fund/Donate to a Project?

  • Click on the Projects Tab

  • Select the project to fund

  • Click on Fund Projects

  • Put in card details


How do I create a poll as an Admin of a Group?

  • Click on the Menu button

  • Select Polls button

  • Click on Create button

  • Select the set to create a poll for

  • Input a title for your poll

  • Input a question

  • Click on Poll duration drop down to select a date

  • Input Choice(s)

  • Click on create poll button

  • Your created poll appears under the ongoing polls tab


How do I vote?

  • Click on the Menu button

  • Select Polls button

  • Click on ongoing polls

  • Select the poll you wish to vote for

  • Select any candidate or project of your choice to vote.

  • Note that users can only vote once.


How can I exit a School Group or School Set?

  • Click on the School tab

  • Select a school group or School set

  • Click on the Menu button at the top right of the screen

  • Click on Group Info or Set Info as the case may be

  • Click on exit group at the button on the page

  • Click on Ok to


How do I remove a member from a School Group as the Admin?

  • Click on the School tab

  • Select a school group or School set

  • Click on the Menu button at the top right of the screen

  • Click on Group Info or Set Info as the case may be

  • Click on a member of the group to remove

  • Select remove member

  • Click Ok to confirm


How do Mute or Unmute a Chat?

  • Click on the group Chat you wish to mute or unmute

  • Click on the Menu button at the top right of the screen

  • Select Mute or Unmute

  • Click Ok to confirm


How do I Block or Unblock a Chat?

  • Click on the group Chat you wish to mute or unmute

  • Click on the Menu button at the top right of the screen

  • Select Mute or Unmute

  • Click Ok to confirm
